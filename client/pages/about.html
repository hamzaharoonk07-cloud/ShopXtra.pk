Our story

Self-care, one ritual at a time

ShopXtra started from a simple observation: the products that matter most in a day aren't one category, they're a rhythm. A hydrating drink before you've had coffee. A rinse of shampoo before the day gets away from you. A shade of lipstick before you leave the house in the evening. Five categories, used at different hours, that had never been sold as a single ritual — until now.

We source electrolytes, coffee, shampoo, soap, and cosmetics that share one idea: premium shouldn't mean complicated. Soft, considered, and priced for daily use across Pakistan.
